A short list of books recommended by Alex Stepanov for civilizing programmers.

Humanities

  • Homer
    • The Iliad. Translated by Rodney Merrill.
      University of Michigan Press, 2009.
    • competent translation in hexameter; matches the sound of the original Greek. As close to the original Greek hexameter as possible in English.
    • http://www.amazon.com/Iliad-New-Translation-Peter-Green/dp/0520281411/
    • Almost like hexameter; but less less dogmatic and rigid
    • Simone Weil - The Iliad, or the poem of Force - http://www.amazon.com/Simone-Weils-Iliad-Poem-Force/dp/0820463612/

    • The Odyssey. Translated by Rodney Merrill.
      University of Michigan Press, 2002.
    • http://www.amazon.com/Odyssey-Homer-Bantam-Classics/dp/0553213997/
    • Mandelbaum: free verse, but not as free as Fagles

  • Plato
    • Plato: Complete Works.
      Edited by John M. Cooper and D. S. Hutchinson. Hackett Publishing Co., 1997.
    • The Dialogues of Plato, Volume 2: The Symposium. Translated by R. E. Allen.
      Yale University Press, 1993.
      This version is really good for Symposium, a dialogue on the nature of love, which is perhaps the most beautiful book every written.

    • Plutarch
      • Plutarch's Lives, Dryden Edition. Volume 1 and Volume 2.
        Edited by Arthur Hugh Clough. Modern Library Classics, 2001.

    • Bible
      • The New Oxford Annotated Bible with the Apocrypha, Revised Standard Version, Expanded Edition.
        Edited by Bruce M. Metzger and Herbert G. May. Oxford University Press, Revised Standard edition, 1977.

    • Shakespeare
      • RSC Shakespeare - http://www.amazon.com/RSC-Shakespeare-Varios/dp/0230003508/
      • Modern Library

    Mathematics

    • Euclid
      • The Thirteen Books of the Elements, Vol. 1: Books 1-2. Translated by Sir Thomas L. Heath.
        Dover Publications, 2nd edition, 1956.
      • The Thirteen Books of the Elements, Vol. 2: Books 3-9. Translated by Sir Thomas L. Heath.
        Dover Publications, 2nd edition, 1956.
      • The Thirteen Books of the Elements, Vol. 3: Books 10-13. Translated by Sir Thomas L. Heath.
        Dover Publications, 2nd edition, 2010.
      • Everyone must work through at least Book 1.

    • George Chrystal
      • Textbook of Algebra
      • http://www.amazon.com/Algebra-Elementary-Text-Book-2-set/dp/0821819313/
